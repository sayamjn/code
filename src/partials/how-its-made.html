Indulge in our delicious chocolate spread and experience the perfect blend of flavor

  • Start by heating milk in a saucepan without bringing it to a boil. Add sugar and cocoa powder to create a rich chocolate mixture.

  • Meanwhile, prepare a water bath and melt butter on it to add to the chocolate mixture.

  • Combine the milk mixture with the melted butter thoroughly using a silicone spatula until the mixture is uniform and silky.

  • Heat the mixture over low heat, allowing it to barely boil to enhance the flavors.

  • Pour the chocolate spread into containers or molds and allow it to cool and harden before enjoying its deliciousness on your favorite foods.
